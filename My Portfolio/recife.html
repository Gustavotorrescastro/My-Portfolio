Recife de Memórias

What is it about?

"Recife de Memórias" is a sensory interactive game that uses accessible technology to stimulate cognition and affection.

In the city of bridges, Recife de Memórias is also a bridge between past and present — a way to reclaim identities, honor stories, and, above all, care with affection and technology.

How does it Work?

The experience is based on a journey through the most iconic tourist spots in "Bairro do Recife", such as:

  • Marco Zero
  • Cais do Sertão
  • Parque das Esculturas
  • Rua do Bom Jesus
  • Forte do Brum

Through a narration, the player must identify the location being described, find it on the map, and press the button of its corresponding color.

After correctly identifying all 5 described points, the user must repeat the sequence of buttons pressed.

The difference of Recife de Memórias lies in its humanized, intuitive, and culturally rooted nature. More than a game, it is a therapeutic tool that activates short- and long-term memories, stimulates attention, and promotes social interaction in an accessible and meaningful way.
